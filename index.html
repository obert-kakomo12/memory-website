Life & Legacy

A Beautiful Journey

Born in Mafararikwa, Gogo P was one of a pillar. From a young age, she showed a tireless spirit, always working to support, care and provide.

Her life was a testament to the Shona proverb, 'Kugona nhaka huchenjera' that is to leave a legacy is to be wise. Whether in her professional life or her role as a Mother, Grandmother, Sister, Aunt and Relative to many she used her tools to build a foundation of love and unity that we honor today.

"Her work is finished, her tools are clean, and her soul is at rest."
